Rationale

Implementation of BCIT COMP1950 New Layout:

Since we have 2 members in the team, we decide to share the work 50/50 between us. We all contribute in designing and creating the content for the project.
The box and border around the content on the page are removed and replaced with accordion javascript, so the content will display as the flow of the lecture. By doing this, it will not look so overwhelming to the students when they view the site.
Instead of have everything listed on the menu on the side, we move the navigation on the header of the page. The students can just hoover their mouse over to view the sub-menu items.
In addition, we add a sidebar on the left to list all the pages that are related to their current page.
We also want our site to be responsive and printer-friendly, we add media query in our css file to do the job.
We add a calender at the bottom of all pages, so the student can alway know what is next and when they have to hand-in their up-coming homework or assignments.

Obstacles during the process:

As we all have full-time job, it is difficult to meet-up in person. Sometimes, we miss our meeting. However, we will try to catch-up with each others' work online and through git-hub repo.
